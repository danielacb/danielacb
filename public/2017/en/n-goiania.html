N_Goiânia 2014

Introduction

NDesign is a national conference for design students held each year in a different city. It’s a non-profit organization that promotes integration between design students all over the country and deepens the critical debate among scholars of the field.

I was part of the organization and worked with the Visual Identity section that was responsible for all the graphic material that the event needed such as posters, social media artwork, campaign projects etc.

Obs.: the Visual Identity of the event was done by Ramom Madeira and Gabriella Bitencourt.

Editorial Project

To host the event we needed to be elected during the previous year, so a campaign project was made showing the reasons why we should be trusted to host the conference the next year.

Promotional Poster

Before the event happened a few promotional posters were made, I was responsible for one of them. I used images of Goiânia (the host town) separated by the known map drawing of the city center.

Photo credits: Daniel Barros

Poster Circo Farofafá

During the week of the event there were a few parties to help people get to know each other. I design the poster for the circus themed party named Circo Farofafá. The inspiration came from the colorful chalkboard signs that I’ve seen in my childhood.

Checklist

Travels’ Checklists were very common before the previous NDesigns, we did a little improvement by making an online version and adding a forecast to help people from all over the country get ready to our event.
