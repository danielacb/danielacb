Mangiaro Visual Identity

“Mangiaro” is a casual dinning restaurant focused on grilled food, and that was what brought the inspiration for the logo, with horizontal stripes referring to the marks that this type of cooking leaves on the food.

Being a casual dinning type of restaurant meant that it should feel like a comfort place to go have a nice meal with friends for a fair price, and that characteristics had an influenced on the brand color and typography choices.
