>> When I was about 14 years old I learned Photoshop, HTML and CSS to feed a Harry Potter themed blog.

On High School, Art History was one of my favorite classes and it was my Art teacher that told me about the Design career for the first time.

But my family convinced me to apply for a “safer” choice, so I tried studying Computer Science for a semester and didn’t liked it. Then I finally went to Design School.

Freshman year in college

Changing my college degree was the best thing I could have done, I liked it since the very fist day and was lucky enough to get an internship at "3G Convites" right after my freshmen year improving my Photoshop and InDesign skills while designing graduation invitations.

My second internship was at Correios, the company that operates the Brazilian postal service, and then I got a better offer to work with a bigger team at Tribunal Regional Eleitoral, the regional court that is responsible for the control and inspection of the whole electoral process.

In 2013 I was granted a scholarship from the Brazilian government called Brazil Scientific Mobility Program and went to study at Arizona State University where I took classes that weren’t available at my home university, including subjects such as UX Design, Design Thinking and Programming (Javascript).

Horseshoe Bend - Arizona

Halloween 2013

When I got back, in 2014, I joined the N_Goiânia organization and started working for B2 Agência.

The following year was even better because I completed two internships at two of the most important design studios in Goiânia: Zebrabold and Dupplamente.

I graduated in Graphic Design in 2016, also participate in the Abril Training Program and started working at WEEK Agência Digital.

2017 promises to be another important year in my life, now that I moved to a new city.

Abril Trainee Program 2016

Ultimately, in the past few years I’ve been studying User Experience, User Interface Design and front-end development, now I’m currently searching for a more challenging job opportunity to work with Digital Design in São Paulo.
