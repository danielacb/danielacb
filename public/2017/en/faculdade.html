College Assignments

Introduction

I got my Graphic Design degree from Universidade Federal de Goiás (Federal University of Goiás), elected the best Graphic Design school among the public institutions by a publication called Guia do Estudante (Students Guide). But it wasn’t just that, I also took a lot of other courses in other institutions to learn Adobe’s softwares, HTML and CSS.

Preview

Details

Portinari Exposition Poster

It was an exercise from the Brazilian Art History class, we needed to design a poster for a fictitious exposition from a Brazilian artist of our choosing.

I chose Cândido Portinari and his “War and Peace” panels painted for the United Nations Headquarters as a gift from the Brazilian government. I was inspired by its colors contrasts and the fact that they are formed by 28 smaller pieces.

"Delegação 137º" Poster

A poster made to promote a trip to “Caldas Novas”, a city considered by many to be the largest hydro-thermal resort in the world, and also for its extinct volcano. That is why I used harm colors and the fire flames inspired lettering.

Lost Collection

For the Packaging class we could choose a product to work with and my choice was to design a special edition for a Lost Collection of DVDs, inspired by a lot of the events on the TV show story. You can check the entire process here.

"Terra Celta" Album cover

In this exercise we were required to redesign a album cover for a band that our professor randomly chose for each one of us. I end up with a band with an Irish inspired kind of music and most of the lyrics were about beer.

Knowing this, I decided to design the cover to look like the Guinness label, the famous Irish dry stout beer, and added a green background just like a Saint Patrick’s Day flag would be.
