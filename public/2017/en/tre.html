Introduction

“Tribunal Regional Eleitoral de Goiás” (Regional Electoral Courts) is the regional court that is responsible for the control and inspection of the whole electoral process in the state of Goiás.

My team was responsible for designing a variety of graphic material for the electoral process such as posters, banners, certificates etc. We also designed the “Verba Legis”, a magazine with articles and essays about the history, the process and other things involving the elections.
