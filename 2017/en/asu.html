Arizona State University

Introduction

In 2013 I was granted a scholarship from the Brazilian government called Brazil Scientific Mobility Program. The opportunity to study abroad at Arizona State University and live in a completely different country made me a better person and also a better designer.

I took classes that weren’t available at my home university, including subjects such as UX Design, Design Thinking and Programming (Javascript).

Details

Video Bumper

For the Motion Graphics class, our first assignment was to create video bumpers to be placed at the beginning and end of all remaining projects. I wanted to give it the idea that the content was coming from inside the television and I used an old tv so I could play with the noisy/glitch images and sound effects.

Kill Bill 2 Opening Credits

Our Final Project was to use After Effects to recreate the opening title sequence of a movie of our choosing.

I chose Kill Bill 2 because it is one of my favorite movies, it’s known for its bright yellow color, the use of typography, the sound track and also because It could work as a recap sequence from the previous movie.

N_Goiânia app

The final project of the Interaction Design class was to create an mobile app. During that time I was still involved with the N_Goiânia 2014 project and I created what would be the event app interface. I made the mockups with Quartz Composer and Origami in order to learn tools that companies like Facebook and Google used.

It never got to be released because I didn’t have the programming knowledge to do so, but it was a good exercise.
